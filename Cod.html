BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on computer science, software development, and IT skills. It prepares students for careers like software developers or system analysts, typically requiring a 10+2 with at least 45-50% marks. Key subjects include programming, database management, and networking.
